  • A new way of staying safe

    ProtectYU is a free mobile app that helps you better react in case of emergency. When something happens, simply press a button to send alerts to your friends, your network or directly to the emergency service the accident requires. ProtectYU provides rapid intervention within eight minutes of the event.  

  • 360° protection

    ProtectYU is the most comprehensive offer on the market for personal and community protection. With ProtectYU, you can better ensure your safety by having access to unique services in individual and community protection.

  • Michel Juneau-Katsuya, MBA

    Président Directeur-Général Co-fondateur

    Michel Juneau-Katsuya (Photo) A recognized expert in national and international security, Michel Juneau-Katsuya has worked as a criminal investigator, an intelligence officer in counter-espionage and anti-terrorism, and, with the Canadian Security Intelligence Service and Royal Canadian Mounted Police, a strategic expert on global affairs.   • Pierre G. Fillion, MBA

    Président et chef de la direction Co-fondateur

    An experienced manager and administrator recognized for his talents in innovation management and emerging technology R&D, Pierre Fillion is the CEO of the Council of Sustainable Industries and the Fédération des plastiques et alliances composites. In his career, he has won success with innovative industrial and technological competitiveness programs and unique initiatives to improve the strategic positioning of Quebec businesses on international markets. As a former military police officer, Fillion brings a wealth of know-how to ProtectYU.

  • Richard Thouin

    A specialist in risk management, Richard Thouin made his career in the police force, including nearly 20 years with the SPVM’s tactical intervention group as an expert in explosives, armed interventions and hostage rescues. Present at numerous high-risk crime scenes, he is noted for overseeing operations at the September 2006 Dawson College shooting. As commanding officer, he was responsible for police operations throughout the Montreal area and executive consultant to the director’s office in the use of force against the terrorist threat.

  • Michel Wilson

    As a consultant in police matters, Michel Wilson has a solid background in tactical intervention and crisis management. A special weapons and tactics specialist, he has coordinated numerous large-scale operations. As commander of the SPVM’s tactical intervention group and stations 3 and 12, Wilson has led investigating and tactical teams alike in the fight against crime. As squad leader, he has particular expertise in public order services and demonstrations.
